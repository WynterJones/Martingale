Questions, answered calmly

Will I look like I've "had work done"?

That's the one thing we promise you won't. Auré's entire approach is the lightest possible touch — softened, rested, glowing skin that reads as a great night's sleep, not a procedure.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes — your first 45-minute skin consultation is complimentary, with no obligation. You'll leave with a personalised plan and a first-visit treatment offer, whether or not you book that day.

Does it hurt, and is there downtime?

Most of our signature treatments are comfortable with little to no downtime. Where a treatment needs recovery time, your clinician will walk you through exactly what to expect before you commit.

How is the Glow Membership different?

Membership is for clients who want skin health on a rhythm: a monthly signature facial, member pricing on every treatment, seasonal reviews, and priority booking — at $149/month, cancel anytime.

What if a treatment isn't right for me?

Then we'll say so. Every plan is medically supervised, and we'd rather keep you for ten years than sell you the wrong thing once.
